Cookie Policy

Last Updated: 12/24/2025

This Cookie Policy explains how Crowbar uses cookies and similar technologies to provide a secure, skill-based platform experience.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device to remember preferences and enable core website functionality.

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Required for platform operation
  • Analytics Cookies: Help improve skill-based features (opt-in)
  • Functional Cookies: Remember user preferences (opt-in)
  • Marketing Cookies: Used only for skill-based promotions (opt-in)

How We Use Cookies

Cookies help us keep you signed in, track skill-based participation, and improve platform usability. Cookies are not used for gambling, chance-based profiling, or random targeting.

Your Choices

You can accept, reject, or customize cookie preferences using our cookie banner or browser settings. Consent can be withdrawn at any time.

Compliance

We comply with GDPR (EU/UK), DPDP (India), UAE Data Law, and US state regulations. Consent records may be logged for audit purposes.
