This Cookie Policy explains how Portrait Intelligence Lab, operated by Crowbar Ventures Limited, uses cookies and similar technologies when you visit our website at https://portrait-intelligence-lab.vercel.app/ .

This Policy should be read together with our Privacy Policy and Terms of Use.

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile) when you visit a website.

Cookies may be “session cookies” (which are deleted when you close your browser) or “persistent cookies” (which remain on your device for a defined period or until deleted).

2. How We Use Cookies

  • Ensure the website functions correctly
  • Maintain security and accessibility
  • Understand how the website is used so it can be improved

We do not use cookies to deliver advertising, behavioural profiling, or automated decision-making.

3. Types of Cookies We Use

3.1 Strictly Necessary (Essential) Cookies

These cookies are required for the website to operate and cannot be switched off.

  • Security and fraud prevention
  • Session management
  • Accessibility and load balancing

Legal basis: Legitimate interest (UK GDPR Article 6(1)(f)) and the “strictly necessary” exemption under the ePrivacy Directive.

3.2 Analytics Cookies

Analytics cookies help us understand how visitors interact with the website by collecting information in an aggregated and anonymised form.

These cookies are used only if you provide explicit consent.

  • Page visits and navigation patterns
  • Time spent on pages
  • General usage statistics

Legal basis: Consent (UK GDPR Article 6(1)(a)).

3.3 Marketing and Advertising Cookies

Portrait Intelligence Lab does not use marketing, advertising, or behavioural tracking cookies.

3.4 Functional / Preference Cookies

Functional cookies allow the website to remember choices such as language or region preferences.

These cookies are optional and used only if enabled.

Legal basis: Consent.

4. Cookie Consent

7. Third-Party Cookies
