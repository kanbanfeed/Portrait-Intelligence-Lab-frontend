Refund Policy

Last Updated: 12/24/2025

This Refund Policy explains when and how refunds may be considered for purchases made within the Crowbar ecosystem. All activities are strictly skill-based.

1. Membership Purchases

Memberships provide immediate digital access and are generally non-refundable. This includes Discounted, Basic, Pro, and Elite tiers.

2. Skill Event Entries

Credits used to join skill-based events are non-refundable once entry is confirmed. Outcomes depend on user performance, not chance.

3. Exceptions

  • Duplicate or accidental multiple payments
  • Technical issues preventing access after payment
  • Confirmed fraudulent transactions
